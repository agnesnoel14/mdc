The society was established with a larger vision of establishing, constructing, organizing, consolidating, supporting, developing, maintaining and administering of educational insitutions according to the need of the place in any part of India for the benefit of all persons irrespective of caste, creed, race and religion. The Management Council is established in pursuance of the memorandum under the Societies Registration Act(XXI of 1860). The college Rajagiri College of Social Sciences is registered as a non-profit, charitable society dedicated solely to educational purposes under the Travancore-Cochin Literary, Scientific, and Charitable Societies Registration Act of 1955.
